Meet the Coder: My Journey

Hello and welcome to my portfolio, a digital space where I showcase my skills, experience, and love for all things software development.

During my academic career, I've had the unique opportunity to intern at three diverse institutions - Ford Motor Company, uOttawa Department of Family Medicine, and Dominon Voting Systems. These experiences have not only allowed me to understand the practical applications of computer science but also enabled me to grow and nurture my skills in C++, Kotlin, Python, and C#.

At Ford, I delved into the dynamic world of Android applications, developing advanced functionalities for their 3D applications. Meanwhile, at uOttawa, I took a plunge into the immersive realm of virtual reality, designing interactive applications to aid in medical education and patient care. These projects, among others, can be found on my work showcase. Each represents a unique problem I had the opportunity to solve, reflecting my persistent quest for knowledge and a zest for overcoming coding challenges.

Outside the world of algorithms and data structures, you can often find me at the local gym, pushing my physical limits, or exploring the great outdoors on adventurous hikes. I believe that fitness and adventure are metaphors for my approach to coding. Much like the thrill of conquering a challenging hiking trail or surpassing a personal record at the gym, I find joy in solving complex coding challenges and creating innovative software solutions. In every coding problem, I see a new opportunity, a new adventure - an occasion to expand my horizons and push the boundaries of what's possible with code.

The dynamic world of software development is a marathon, not a sprint, and I am committed to continual learning and personal growth within this field. As I navigate my way through this fascinating landscape, I am always eager to collaborate with other tech enthusiasts and contribute to projects that can make a difference.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. It's an honor to share my journey with you, and I eagerly look forward to any potential collaborations or opportunities that may arise.
